Phoebe Philo

As one of the biggest brands in fashion, Phoebe Philo’s departure from French label Celine was certainly a shock for fans of her minimal, ultra wearable designs. And after 10 years of Philo’s singular, authoritative vision for the brand, which was praised by women for its unfussy and relaxed take on clothing and accessories, former Saint Laurent designer Hedi Slimane was announced as her successor in January of this year.
